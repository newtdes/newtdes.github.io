About me
======
Hi everyone, welcome to my personal website. I'm final-year student at [the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology](https://hkust.edu.hk/), where I am studying Computer Science (CSE), Mathematics (MATH), Data Science (DSCT), and General Business Management (GBM). I am also a proud [Saigonese](https://saigoneer.com/saigon-culture/837-the-saigonese)! Click into "Saigonese" to know a brief introduction about my city, [Saigon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ho_Chi_Minh_City)!

My current research interest is about AI for Scientific Discovery, AI4Science. Personally, I am also trying to expand my interest a bit to Computer Vision, so I do spend some time understanding Visual-Language Model recently.

My hobbies (that do not relate to academics at all :P) is to watch movies, read books (that not so many people read :P), and learn languages. Currently, I am learning Mandarin（華語）and Cantonese（廣東話）although my Cantonese does not get any better (updated: I think I am quite good at Taiwanese Mandarin now, but my Cantonese does not get much improvements).

I am also a Poker player, and a fan of boardgame (althought I don't play much :P). In my free time, watching movie is what I normally do, since I believe 「在電影裡，看見自己」(you see yourself inside the movie).
